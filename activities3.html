Activities

There are many planned activities at all the cliff sites. Some are scheduled daily, weekly, monthly and yearly. These activities are not however run by the state park and recreational department. They are operated and manage by private vendors but are partners with the state department. Therefore, for schedule i nquiries or information, please contact them directly. For a list of activities, please refer to the table below:

Located within a 100 mile radius from Irvine, California, they can be reached by roads easily. The closest cliff to Irvine is West Cliff, which is the most popular and most visited.
